Add new account

Add a new Twitter account to allow other users to request to post to the account, pending approval. Adding an account also allows you to configure automatic retweeting of @mentions, with a sentiment filter enabled. Nothing will be posted to this account without your consent, and you can remove the account at any time either in this app or via your Twitter application permissions. If you are currently logged into Twitter, that account will automatically be authenticated upon pressing the button below. Log out of Twitter or sign into a different account to add a different handle.
