In progress

  • Tweet management: Tweet rejection, modifying date/time of a scheduled tweet, icon changes.

  • Account management: Public vs private twitter accounts so you can add your own personal twitter account, but only you have access.

  • Retweet authorized mentions: Mentions from portal-based accounts are automatically added to the management queue.

Future development

  • User voice: Add Uservoice functionality to solicit feedback and improve app.

  • Email management: Approve or reject tweets from email; possibly modify tweets from email.

  • Notification management: Decide what type of notifications you want sent to your email, and on what schedule.

  • Retweet mentions: Mentions from any account are automatically added to the management queue.

  • UX Improvements: Tabs for the tweet queue, rejected tweets, a table to allow you to easily compare values.

  • Twitter logout: Allows you to log out of your currently logged-in twitter account from portal.

  • Security group access: Allow anyone from a security group to manage a twitter account (multiple people can approve tweets).

  • Content tweet automation: Automatically tweet out new articles based on repo and handle.

  • Link shortener: Similar to bit.ly, a link shortener that is less controlled than aka.ms.
